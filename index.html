Education

2016-2021

Polytech Tours, Université du Québec à Chicoutimi

France, Canada

I joined the Polytech preparatory classes (PeiP) in 2016, where I completed two years of coursework mainly focused on mathematics, with an introduction to computer science. After that, I was able to join the computer science department at Polytech Tours, where I completed two years of the engineering curriculum. I then had the opportunity to take part in an exchange program with the Université du Québec à Chicoutimi, which offers a double degree through a one-year master’s program in computer science, with a specialization in game development. Throughout these years of study, I completed two internships. The first took place in the IT department of MATCH supermarkets in Lille, France, where I contributed to the redesign of the self-checkout system. Then, after completing my master’s degree at UQAC, I joined the Faculty of Music at Université Laval to develop serious games aimed at helping young musicians learn to play different instruments.
